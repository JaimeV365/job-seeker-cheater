# Legal Disclaimer -- Job Seeker Cheater

**Last updated:** February 2026

## Nature of the Tool

Job Seeker Cheater is a **job-suggestion tool**. It aggregates publicly available
job listings from third-party APIs and ranks them against your CV and preferences
using algorithmic scoring.

## What This Tool Does NOT Do

- **Does not apply for jobs on your behalf.**
- **Does not guarantee job matches are accurate, current, or complete.**
- **Does not provide employment, legal, or career advice.**
- **Does not verify the legitimacy of job listings.**
- **Does not establish any employment relationship.**

## User Responsibilities

By using this tool, you acknowledge that:

1. You are solely responsible for verifying job listings before applying.
2. You are solely responsible for any applications you submit.
3. Job listings are sourced from third-party APIs and may be outdated, inaccurate,
   or removed at any time.
4. Match scores are algorithmic estimates, not guarantees of suitability.
5. You should independently verify salary, location, and other job details.

## Third-Party Data Sources

Job listings are fetched from:

- **Remotive** (remotive.com) -- Subject to Remotive's terms of service.
- **Arbeitnow** (arbeitnow.com) -- Subject to Arbeitnow's terms of service.

This tool uses these APIs' public endpoints in compliance with their published

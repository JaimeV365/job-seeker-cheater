# Job Seeker Cheater -- Brand Guide

## Brand Personality

**Cheeky. Confident. On your side.**

Job Seeker Cheater is the tool that does the boring job-hunting legwork so you
can spend your time on things that matter -- like the beach, gaming, or literally
anything else. The brand voice is irreverent but trustworthy: we joke about
laziness while delivering genuinely useful results.

**Tagline options:**
- "Your laptop works. You relax."
- "Let the machine do the hustling."
- "Cheat code for job seekers."

## Imagery Guidelines

- **Hero images**: People visibly relaxing (beach, sofa, hammock, gaming) with a
  laptop nearby that is clearly "doing the work."
- **Tone**: Warm, aspirational, slightly mischievous.
- **Sources** (free, no attribution required for Unsplash):
  - Beach + laptop scenes
  - Couch gaming sessions
  - Coffee shop "pretend working"
- **Do NOT use**: Corporate stock, suit-and-tie handshakes, sad job seekers.

## UI Components Style

- **Buttons**: Rounded corners (8px), purple bg, white text, gold hover glow
- **Cards**: White bg, subtle shadow, 12px radius, left purple accent bar
- **Score badges**: Circular, gold bg with deep blue text
- **Tags/Skills**: Pill-shaped, lavender bg, deep blue text
- **Match reasons**: Green checkmark icon + text
- **Gap warnings**: Amber triangle icon + text

## Logo

MVP uses text-based logo:
- "Job Seeker **Cheater**" -- "Cheater" in Bordeaux Red, rest in Deep Blue
- Optional: small beach umbrella emoji or gamepad icon as wordmark
